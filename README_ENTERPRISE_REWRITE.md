# Enterprise positioning rewrite

This branch repositions Vektas for mid-market and enterprise organizations.

Primary changes:

- Broaden positioning from private AI consulting to AI systems engineering, workflow modernization, systems integration, enterprise knowledge systems, and custom software.
- Replace small-business-oriented messaging with complex operational and enterprise use cases.
- Introduce the engineering principle: use AI where reasoning adds value and deterministic software where reliability and repeatability matter.
- Replace pricing language with an enterprise engagement model: assessment and discovery, pilot and validation, production implementation, and optimization and support.
- Rewrite About and CTA copy for senior technical and business buyers.
- Update route metadata for the new positioning.
- Remove unsupported testimonial content from the active homepage path.

No customer results, certifications, or case studies were invented.
