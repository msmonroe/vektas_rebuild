Prince Puckler's has been part of Eugene life for generations. Students, families and longtime regulars gather around a simple ritual: choose a flavor, add a warm cone, and enjoy it before the Oregon weather changes its mind.

The website should make that visit easier by putting flavors, hours, pies and directions exactly where customers expect them.
