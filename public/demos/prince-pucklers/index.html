A Eugene tradition

Made here. Loved here.

Prince Puckler's has been scooping rich, creamy ice cream in Eugene for generations. The flavors are made locally with quality ingredients, then served in a neighborhood shop where the line is part of the ritual.

Regulars know their order. First-timers face the delicious problem of choosing among dozens of flavors.

A scoop of history

The day the Obamas stopped by.

Barack and Michelle Obama visited Prince Puckler's on May 17, 2008. The neighborhood quickly gathered, and Mint Chip gained an enduring unofficial nickname: “Obama.”
