Frequently asked

Before you arrive

Are dairy-free choices available?

Yes. The shop rotates several dairy-free sorbets, alongside low-fat frozen yogurt selections. Availability varies.

Can I order ice cream for a party?

Hand-packed containers and larger tubs are available. Call the shop for current options and lead times.

Can I order a custom ice cream pie?

Custom pies can be made with two flavors and toppings. The official site asks customers to allow at least 48 hours.

Is this the official Prince Puckler's website?

No. This is a clearly labeled redesign concept created by Vektas to demonstrate a possible modern customer experience.
